Kim Daniel

Birth Name: Kim Daniel (김다니엘)

Role: Guitar, Vocal | Composer and Writer

Birthday: March 18, 1997 | ♓ Pisces | Born in France

Height: 175 cm | Weight: 74 kg


Facts:

  • He' s not just a musician; he's the mastermind behind the band's sound!
  • Part of The Poles, and also the front person.
  • Brown toy poodle = Poong | “Mononoke Hime"
  • He says that he first decides on the title while writing, then sets the speaker to write a story like a novel. He usually writes the lyrics and melody almost at the same time.
  • He has a bike, Daelim Honda DH88.
  • From time to time, he would work as their own stylist.
